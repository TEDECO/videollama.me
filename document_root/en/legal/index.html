4. Addressee of assignments or data transfer

Due to the nature of the service, we use the meet.jit.si infrastructure, through its API, with the configuration optiondisableThirdPartyRequests not to use Google Analytics tracking options that otherwise are activated by default.

Once you get into the room, you are into their servers, whose privacy policy is https://jitsi.org/meet-jit-si-privacy/

The rest of libraries are loaded from our server, without using any CDNs or third partie servers

5. Cookies5. Cookies

We use our own cookie where we store the identifiers of the rooms you have entered from that device, which serves both to be able to show them to you on the main page, and not to show you the instructions to configure the device every time you enter.

All the embedded videos are made with youtube-nocookie, so there is not tracking by third parties until the video is played.

We do not use any additional cookies, neither our own nor those of third parties.
